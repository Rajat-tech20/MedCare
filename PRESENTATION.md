## **Slide 3: Introduction**

### **Project Overview**

MedCare is a comprehensive, web-based platform designed to modernize pharmacy operations.

**Key Goals:**

- **Bridge the Gap:** Connect customers directly with pharmacy inventory.
- **Streamline Operations:** Simplify specific tasks like prescription uploads and inventory tracking.
- **Enhance Experience:** Provide a seamless, e-commerce-like shopping experience for medicines.

---

## **Slide 4: Key Features & Modules**

We have designed 5 core modules to handle different aspects of the system:

1.  **Authentication (Security)**
2.  **Shop & Product Discovery (Customer)**
3.  **Cart & Checkout (Transaction)**
4.  **Prescription Management (Medical)**
5.  **Admin Dashboard (Management)**

---

## **Slide 5: Module 1 - Authentication**

### **Secure Access Control**

- **Role-Based Login:** Separate access for _Admins_ and _Customers_.
- **Safety:** Ensures unauthorized users cannot access sensitive admin panels.
- **User Experience:** Directs users to the right dashboard immediately after login.

---

## **Slide 6: Module 2 - Shop & Discovery**

### **Finding Medicines Made Easy**

- **Visual Catalog:** Clean grid layout with product images and prices.
- **Smart Filtering:** Sidebar filters for categories like _Pain Relief_, _First Aid_, etc.
- **Sorting Options:** Sort by Price (Low/High) to find the best deals.
- **Search:** Quick search functionality.

---

## **Slide 7: Module 3 - Cart & Checkout**

### **Seamless Transactions**

- **Persistent Cart:** Cart items are saved even if you close the browser (Local Storage).
- **Interactive Drawer:** Slide-out cart for quick reviews and quantity adjustments.
- **Secure Payment Flow:**
  - Dedicated checkout page.
  - **Visual Trust:** Custom "Razorpay" style animation with a 3D coin flip to simulate secure processing.

---

## **Slide 8: Module 4 - Prescription Upload**

### **Digitizing Prescriptions**

- **Upload Interface:** Specialized drag-and-drop zone for prescription files.
- **Validation:** Checks for valid file formats (JPG, PNG, PDF).
- **Instant Feedback:** "Toast" notifications confirm successful uploads to the user.

---

## **Slide 9: Module 5 - Admin Dashboard**

### **Business Control Center**

- **Quick Stats:** View Total Sales, Active Orders, and Low Stock alerts at a glance.
- **Inventory Management:** Add, edit, or remove medicines from the store.
- **User Oversight:** View list of registered customers.

---

## **Slide 10: Technology Stack**

### **Built With Modern Web Standards**

- **HTML5:** Semantic structure.
- **CSS3:** Custom variables, Flexbox/Grid layouts, Keyframe Animations.
- **JavaScript (ES6+):** Dynamic logic for cart, filtering, and interactions.
- **Tools:** Phosphor Icons, Google Fonts (Inter).
